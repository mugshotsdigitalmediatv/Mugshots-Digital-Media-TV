Manila Times Economic Forum 2023

Manila Times Economic Forum 2023 Maintaining Momentum Gearing for Growth proudly state for the first time to have an in-person event together with some important people in the industry after three years of global pandemic. Analysts, Economists, great speakers from private sectors and public sectors greatly shared good news that the economy is on its peak. The Manila Times Business Forum for 2023 has been a success.

Expo Maritime Philippines 2023

Expo Maritime Philippines 2023 Flag State and Ship Builders highlight the best practices of the World's Largest Flag States and Registries and present the potentials of growing ship building and ship repair industry. By this, the event promotes Maritime safety and security, as well to protect the marine environment. Through Flag States and Registries, visitors can gain knowledge because it includes exhibit and seminar for stake holders from different industries. There's also an onsite job recruitment of different Manning Agencies Seminars for Maritime courses, Seafarers, Health and Wellness, Business Franchising Start-Up, Entrepreneurship and Financial Literacy for Marines, and for their family.

43rd National Conference for Employers

Following the lessons learned from the implementation of the “1 Million Jobs Project '' spearheaded by ECOP in 2021, the 2022 National Conference of Employers (NCE) will zero in on “employment generation as a recovery strategy”. On its 43rd NCE, ECOP and its constituents in the employer sector, policy-makers, development partners, social partners, and other stakeholders will flesh out relevant employment issues, perspectives, and recommendations.
